    <h2 id="April_13th.2C_Wednesday" class="gh-sectionHeader"><a class="gh-miniButton gh-miniButtonBlack" style="float: right;" href="/wikis/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/edit/1/April_13th">Edit</a>April 13th, Wednesday<br></h2>

     <b>Weather: </b>Cloudy
     <br>

    <div class="gh-red-box">
     <b>Social Links Available</b>:&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 
     <p><i>None.</i> Event scene with Yosuke, Chie, and Saki Konishi. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </p>
     <p><b>Lecture:</b> Listen to Morooka shower you with insults. Knowledge increase. </p> 
    </div> 
    <h3 id="Early_Morning" class="gh-sectionHeader"><a class="gh-miniButton gh-miniButtonBlack" style="float: right;" href="/wikis/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/edit/2/April_13th">Edit</a>Early Morning<br></h3> 
    <p>The day begins on your walk to school. The same student that crashed the previous morning crashes again and manages to end up inside of a trash can. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/b/b9/2013-01-09-110429.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-110429.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-110429.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-110429.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>Help him get himself free and he will introduce himself as <a href="/wikis/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/Yosuke" title="Yosuke" class="mw-redirect">Yosuke Hanamura</a>. Say, "<b>Nice to meet you, too!</b>" </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/1/1d/2013-01-09-110450.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-110450.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-110450.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-110450.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>Yosuke starts discussing the weird accident yesterday with the body hanging from the antenna. He asks if you think it was some kind of warning. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/e/e8/2013-01-09-110535.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-110535.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-110535.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-110535.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>Respond with, "<b>You might be right.</b>" You both hasten off to school together on Yosuke's bike so as not to be late. </p> 
    <h3 id="Morning" class="gh-sectionHeader"><a class="gh-miniButton gh-miniButtonBlack" style="float: right;" href="/wikis/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/edit/3/April_13th">Edit</a>Morning<br></h3> 
    <p>The scene cuts to your class sitting in homeroom listening to Morooka lecture you and shower you with insults. Bear with it for a couple of lines, laugh if you wish, and then you'll receive a <b>Knowledge</b> increase. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/9/9d/2013-01-09-110649.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-110649.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-110649.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-110649.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <h3 id="After_School" class="gh-sectionHeader"><a class="gh-miniButton gh-miniButtonBlack" style="float: right;" href="/wikis/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/edit/4/April_13th">Edit</a>After School<br></h3> 
    <p>The game cuts to After School (which is when you will normally be allowed freedom to do whatever you wish, but another automated scene takes place today. </p>
    <p>Yosuke comes up to you and asks if you're getting used to Inaba. Choose whichever option you wish. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/d/d8/2013-01-09-110730.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-110730.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-110730.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-110730.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>He'll outline the benefits of living in a small town and remind you that Inaba is known for its grilled steak, which he snidely remarks is nothing really special to be proud of. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/c/c2/2013-01-09-110826.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-110826.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-110826.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-110826.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p><a href="/wikis/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/Chie" title="Chie" class="mw-redirect">Chie</a> and <a href="/wikis/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/Yukiko" title="Yukiko" class="mw-redirect">Yukiko</a>, the girls in green and red from yesterday, walk over to the two of you. Chie starts heckling Yosuke about how he owes her for breaking her copy of <i>Trial of the Dragon</i>, insisting that Yosuke treat her to some of the same steak he was just making fun of. Chie tries to pull Yukiko into the planned blackmail but Yukiko claims she has to head off to the Inn to work and leaves the room. </p> 
    <h3 id="Junes.2C_Food_Court" class="gh-sectionHeader"><a class="gh-miniButton gh-miniButtonBlack" style="float: right;" href="/wikis/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/edit/5/April_13th">Edit</a>Junes, Food Court<br></h3> 
    <p>The scene cuts to Junes with Chie and yourself sitting at a table. Yosuke walks up carrying a tray with three meals on it. Chie greedily takes her share and teases him a little more for taking her to "his place," saying that it doesn't really count as payback. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/d/d6/2013-01-09-111013.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-111013.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-111013.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-111013.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>Yosuke takes this opportunity to clarify any confusion you might have as to what Chie means by telling you that he actually just recently moved from the city to Inaba, himself, about six months ago so his dad could manage the Junes. Chie snidely remarks that Junes has damaged the town and destroyed the small stores in the Shopping District. </p>
    <p>A girl enters on the other side of the food court. Yosuke notices her, gets excited, and says that it's Saki-senpai. He rushes over to her and starts conversing. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/a/a6/2013-01-09-111215.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-111215.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-111215.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-111215.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>Ask Chie, "<b>Who's that?</b>" and she'll tell you that the girl is <a href="/wikis/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/Saki_Konishi" title="Saki Konishi">Saki Konishi</a>, whose family owns the local liquor store in the <a href="/wikis/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/Shopping_District,_North" title="Shopping District, North" class="mw-redirect">Shopping District</a>. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/6/63/2013-01-09-111250.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-111250.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-111250.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-111250.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>Yosuke comments that she looks really tired and Saki teases him for a few minutes about bringing his friends to Junes to boost their business. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/5/52/2013-01-09-111320.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-111320.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-111320.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-111320.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>She's clearly not happy that Junes is putting her family's store out of business. You also find out that Saki is working part-time at Junes to help out. Saki then sees you and Chie and walks over to the table. </p>
    <p>She introduces herself and continues to tease Yosuke by saying that it's nice the two of you are getting along since he doesn't have many friends. Saki says "Hana-chan" is a good guy, but he can be a bit nosy. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/b/bd/2013-01-09-111507.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-111507.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-111507.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-111507.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>Tell Saki that, "<b>Nah, he's a good guy.</b>" and she'll tell you she's just kidding-- she knows he is. Saki realizes what time it is and excuses herself to go to work. </p>
    <p>Chie hassles Yosuke about his obvious affection for Saki, relating it to the forbidden love of Romeo and Juliet. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/d/d4/2013-01-09-111613.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-111613.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-111613.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-111613.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>She then gets onto the topic of the <b>Midnight Channel</b> and the rumors surrounding it regarding soulmates. The three of you agree to try it out some time. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/a/a8/2013-01-09-111635.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-111635.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-111635.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-111635.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <h3 id="Evening" class="gh-sectionHeader"><a class="gh-miniButton gh-miniButtonBlack" style="float: right;" href="/wikis/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/edit/6/April_13th">Edit</a>Evening<br></h3> 
    <p>Back at home, you're having dinner in front of the TV with Nanako again. Ask her, "<b>Did your father call?</b>" and she'll tell you that she didn't, but she's clearly worried. </p>
    <p>There's a noise at the door and Dojima walks in, exhausted. He tells you that work was really busy and joins you in front of the TV as well. A follow-up segment about Mayumi Yamani (the dead TV reporter) comes on and confirms Yamano was in an affair with Taro Namatame, the husband of Misuzu Hiiragi, a famous singer. The announcer states that there are no suspects in the case. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/5/5b/2013-01-09-111854.jpg/468px-2013-01-09-111854.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-09-111854.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-09-111854.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>A Junes commercial comes on and Nanako sings along. You turn around and see that Dojima has already fallen asleep. </p>
    <p>You'll be given control again; save our game at the calendar. Go to the stairs and choose "<b>Watch TV in your room.</b>" </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/1/1f/2013-01-13-103850.jpg/468px-2013-01-13-103850.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-13-103850.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-13-103850.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p>
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/a/ae/2013-01-13-103919.jpg/468px-2013-01-13-103919.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-13-103919.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-13-103919.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>A slick animated cutscene will come on, showcasing the rain and lovely lighting. You seemingly give up hope on the midnight TV idea and turn away when it automatically turns on. A disembodied voice says, "I am thou, thou art I..." etc. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/5/5c/2013-01-13-103941.jpg/468px-2013-01-13-103941.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-13-103941.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-13-103941.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>You touch the TV screen and your hand actually goes into the TV itself. Then half of your body gets sucked inside. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/6/69/2013-01-13-104055.jpg/468px-2013-01-13-104055.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-13-104055.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-13-104055.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>The cutscene ends and your upper torso is sitll inside the TV. You manage to pull yourself out and fall to the floor, making a loud noise. </p>
    <p>Nanako calls to you from your door asking if you're okay. Say, "<b>Did I wake you up?</b>" She'll say she heard a noise and that'll be the end of it. She goes to bed and you decide to talk with the others about it tomorrow. </p>
