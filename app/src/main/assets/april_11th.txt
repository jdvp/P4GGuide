    <h2 id="April_11th.2C_Monday" class="gh-sectionHeader">April 11th, Monday<br></h2> 
    <div class="gh-red-box">
     <b>Weather: </b>Cloudy&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

    <h2 id="Afternoon" class="gh-sectionHeader">Afternoon</h2> 
    <h3 id="Arrival" class="gh-sectionHeader">Arrival</h3> 
    <p>You arrive at the Inaba train station on Monday, April 11th, 2011, in the <b>Afternoon</b>. (Time of day is very important in Persona 4 -- many events are only available during certain times.) </p>
    <p>A man comes up to you and introduces himself as Ryotaro Dojima, your mother's younger brother. So, your uncle. You'll be staying with him over the course of the school year. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/1/1a/2013-01-02-110050.jpg/468px-2013-01-02-110050.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-02-110050.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-02-110050.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>Dojima makes a few awkward comments about changing your diapers when you were young and then introduces his daughter, Nanako Dojima, who seems extremely shy at first but mananges a "...'lo." </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/f/f0/2013-01-02-110151.jpg/468px-2013-01-02-110151.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-02-110151.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-02-110151.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>As Dojima and Nanako walk over to his car, an Unfriendly-looking Girl passes by you, then halts, turns around and hands you a slip of paper that she says you've dropped. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/9/92/2013-01-02-110243.jpg/468px-2013-01-02-110243.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-02-110243.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-02-110243.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>The paper was Dojima's address you had written down at some point. A dialogue option will appear, but it doesn't matter which you choose: the girl brushes off your thanks or denial either way. She walks away and you get in the car with Dojima and Nanako. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/3/37/2013-01-03-101252.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-101252.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-101252.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-101252.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p>
    <p style="text-align: center"></p> 
    <h3 id="A_Strange_Encounter_and_Exploration" class="gh-sectionHeader">A Strange Encounter and Exploration</h3> 
    <p>On the way home, Dojima stops at the gas station. Nanako goes to the bathroom and Dojima goes to smoke while the attendant fills up the car and tells you there's a part-time job open at the station. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/a/aa/2013-01-02-110401.jpg/468px-2013-01-02-110401.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-02-110401.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-02-110401.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>When Nanako and Dojima come back, they comment that you're looking ill. Say you're just tired, or any of the other options and you'll be able to walk around for a bit, exploring the Central Shopping District, South. </p>
    <p>The first thing you'll notice is a shimmering blue butterfly, wisping around on the far side of the street. These butterflies (along with the calendar at the Dojima Residence) mark Save Points. Walk up to the butterfly and press X to check and save your game. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/2/28/2013-01-02-110726.jpg/468px-2013-01-02-110726.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-02-110726.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-02-110726.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>You'll also find the Unfriendly-looking girl from the train station in the street as well. You can talk with her, but she won't really remember you. For now. You can tell her that you just met her or if you ask her what she was doing at the station. All options lead nowhere. </p>
    <p>Look around for a bit and get a sense of what shops are in the area. In order from south to north, you'll find the Yomenaido Bookstore (where you'll be able to purchase books to read at night and increase stats), Daidara Metalworks (where you'll come to sell various items, buy new weapons and armor), the Mailbox, Marukyu Tofu, as well as a Capsule and Vending Machine in front of the Shiroku Store (where you'll later be able to exchange <b>Tatsuhime Ladybugs</b> for <b>Bread Crumbs</b> and various gemstones for items). </p>
    <p>You won't be able to enter the Central Shopping District, North just yet. </p>
    <p>Save your game before talking with Dojima again to leave the Shopping District. You'll be given a Help message telling you that pressing Select during a conversation will allow you to see the Chat Log in case you've sped through the text and missed something important. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/4/40/2013-01-03-102102.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-102102.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-102102.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-102102.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <h2 id="Evening" class="gh-sectionHeader">Evening</h2> 
    <h3 id="The_Dojima_Residence" class="gh-sectionHeader">The Dojima Residence</h3> 
    <p>You arrive at the Dojima Residence and sit down to eat dinner with the other two. Dojima starts talking and you find out that your parents have begun working overseas and that's why you've come to Inaba and are staying with Dojima. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/e/e4/2013-01-03-102145.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-102145.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-102145.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-102145.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>Dojima gets a call in the middle of dinner and has to depart, leaving you and Nanako home alone together. In order to strike-up a conversation, you're given the option of asking her what her dad does, remarking that it must be hard for her to be without a mother, or to just sit in silence. Choose "What does your dad do?" </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/a/ad/2013-01-03-102452.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-102452.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-102452.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-102452.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>Nanako explains that her father is a detective for the police department. The TV then interrupts the conversation and a reporter on the channel begins discussing a recent affair scandal regarding an enka singer and a fellow news reporter, Mayumi Yamano. Nanako gets bored and changes the channel to a Junes commercial and sings along. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/f/fe/2013-01-03-102621.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-102621.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-102621.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-102621.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>Finally, you are given control again, standing in the Living Room. Take a peek around. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/8/82/2013-01-03-102758.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-102758.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-102758.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-102758.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>You'll notice the <b>Calendar </b>on the wall acts as a Save Point. There's also a <b>Fridge</b> in the kitchen that you can open and find <b>Fried Rice</b>. Select the "Eat it." option. Nothing happens, but you'll be able to check for food and eat said food on certain days for Courage stat increases. If you check the <b>Kitchen</b>, you just notice how clean and organized it is. Talking with Nanako will suggest that you leave her alone. </p>
    <p><i><b>Save your game before going to your room!</b></i> It will be quite a while before you can save again. Make sure you have 30-40 minutes and a charged Vita battery. </p>
    <p>Go to the <b>Stairs</b> and enter <b>Your Room</b>. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/2/2b/2013-01-03-102850.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-102850.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-102850.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-102850.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>There's a TV, a Study Desk, a Sofa, and a Futon, but attempting to check any of these will result in a message informing you that you're tired and suggesting you go to sleep. Check the Futon. When prompted whether to "Sleep?" select "Yes." </p>
    <p>The screen will go black and you'll awaken in a foggy dreamstate. </p> 
    <h2 id="Dreamstate" class="gh-sectionHeader">Dreamstate</h2> 
    <h3 id="Foggy_Dreamstate" class="gh-sectionHeader">Foggy Dreamstate<br></h3> 
    <p>The game will dump you into an area much like this one: </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/0/09/2013-01-03-103012.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-103012.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-103012.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-103012.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>Start running forward along the red path in front of you. (Note: camera inversion settings can be changed by pressing ∆ (Triangle) and scrolling to Config -&gt; Inversion. </p>
    <p>You'll hear a voice from ahead asking if "you seek the truth?" Keep moving forward. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/4/4d/2013-01-03-103109.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-103109.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-103109.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-103109.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>The voice will come again and say, "If it's the truth you desire, come and find me." Keep running. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/1/1c/2013-01-03-103122.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-103122.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-103122.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-103122.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>You will eventually come to a door-like portal thing. Press X to check it. </p> 
    <span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/3/3c/2013-01-03-103151.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-103151.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-103151.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-103151.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span> 
    <p>You're thrown into a defensive/attack stance, grasping a massive katana that came from nowhere, with a shadowy figure standing in front of you and a combat HUD covering your screen. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/8/82/2013-01-03-103234.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-103234.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-103234.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-103234.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>Choosing Attack or Skill -&gt; Zio/Cleave will damage the shadow, but it will merely keep antagonizing you. After three attacks, the figure will tell you that the truth will be hard to find. </p> 
    <p style="text-align: center"><span class="thumb tnone"><span class="thumbinner"><img alt="" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/shin-megami-tensei-persona-4-golden/thumb/f/f1/2013-01-03-103309.jpg/468px-2013-01-03-103309.jpg" data-original="" width="100%" height="" data-width="468" data-height="265" _imagename="2013-01-03-103309.jpg" _imagecaption="2013-01-03-103309.jpg" class="" max-width="100%"></span></span></p> 
    <p>Attack twice more with anything; both will miss and then you'll be dismissed; the screen will turn black and you awaken on Tuesday, April 12th, with Nanako calling you for breakfast. </p>
